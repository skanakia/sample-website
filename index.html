Delhi-Belly

Delhi-Belly is a modern restaurant that offers authentic Indian street food. With 2 Locations, one in Chevy Chase and one in Foggy Bottom, Delhi-Belly offers a progressive take on classic Indian Cuisine. Chef, Hemant Kanakia, re-imagined his menu in order to place a heavy emphasis on presentation and affordability. The portions are small as this approach mirrors Spanish tapas in that many dishes are ordered and shared amongst the table.
